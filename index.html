SkullOS
Homebrew OS Docs

SkullOS is an open-source, unconventional operating system (OS) deliberately built in contrast to the norms and design principles of modern operating systems. The project focuses on simplicity, transparency, and full control over the system, rather than abstraction and user convenience.
